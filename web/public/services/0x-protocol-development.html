Frequently Asked Questions


1 What is Zrx (0x) protocol?

ZRX is an open-source protocol built on the ethereum blockchain that enables the peer-to-peer exchange of ethereum-based tokens. It is also referred to as a decentralized exchange (DEX).

2 What does the ZRX coin do?

0x protocol’s native token (ZRX) is used for two primary reasons. The first is to pay fees to the Relayers – those who create a decentralized Cryptocurrency exchange using 0x protocol. The second use case is for the decentralized governance of the protocol. This means that those who hold ZRX tokens are given complete control as to how the 0x protocol changes over time.

3 What are the use cases of ZRX?

The ZRX is a pluggable building block for DApps that requires exchange functionality, and that’s why there are so many good use cases surrounding its use: Decentralized Governance, Prediction Market, Stable Tokens , Decentralized Loans, Fund Management. Apart from that using 0x protocol, the fact that one can do wallet to wallet trading without having to send their tokens to a traditional centralized exchange first is a gaming changing integration
