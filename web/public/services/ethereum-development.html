Applications that can be build on Ethereum Blockchain

Any application that serves a specific purpose to its users and has an important property that the application itself does not depend on any specific existing party can be built on ethereum. Some potential possible applications includes applications for identity management, crowd-funding, decentralised token exchanges, decentralised Lending, decentralised advertisement, decentralized prediction market, decentralized governance platforms etc.

Our Ethereum Development Services

Permission Blockchain

Our uphold in understanding the ethereum blockchain give us upper hand to guide our clients to fork and configure ethereum blockchain specific to their business logic and most relevant consensus algorithm chosen from Geth based POA, Parity based POA, Quorum based IBFT, Raft to name a few.

Smart Contract Development

Smart Contracts are very crucial part in the blockchain, with our solidity experts we provide full stack smart contract development, consulting and auditing, held experience in building smart contracts for fundraising, games, collectibles, identity, marketplaces, tokenized securities, lending and subscriptions makes us the best in the blockchain business.

Smart Contracts Audits

Security flaws, misbehavior and inefficiency in smart contract code can potentially be very expensive when one deploys a smart contract onto the blockchain. With QuillAudits we help our clients identify potential threats prevailing in their smart contract code and make them more secure.

Decentralised Exchange Development

We build decentralized token exchange comparable to Radar Relay(0x), OasisDEX (MakerDAO). Our exchanges are highly secure & decentralized with some key features including fully customizable layout , embedded automated revenue system and modular admin panels.

dApp Development

Our team has expertise in developing custom secure web dapps and mobile dapps with integrated smart contracts and 2nd layer decentralised storage (IPFS, BigchainDB) that provides an interface kit to manage the lifecycle of the blockchain and maintain a database of people in the ecosystem.

ICO/STO Development

Using our token Issuance platform our clients can create Ethereum token contracts and crowdsale contracts. Our platform empowers clients to issue tokens, create and run customized bounty and airdrop programs, interact with smart contracts and manage investors and users.

TokenBridge

With TokenBridge, our clients can leverage a sidechain for faster transactions and cheaper gas charges. They can simply shift their circulation of tokens from their main net, where transactions cost more and take more time to complete, and seamlessly move them over to a sidechain

Wallets

Our Ethereum development expertise lies in building web, mobile and desktop based multi-signature wallets. We develop wallets compatible with native ethereum coin, including custom coins and tokens.

Frequently Asked Questions


1 Where Ethereum Blockchain can be used?

Ethereum Blockchain is intended to provide Blockchain tools for developers to build there own decentralized apps on the networks. To Know more about Ethereum, click here

2 How do Ethereum smart contracts works?

“Smart contracts” works like a set of agreements embedded in computer codes that facilitate the exchange of money, content, property, shares, or anything of value. And smart contract when running on the blockchain becomes like a self-operating computer program that automatically executes when specific conditions are met, and they run exactly as programmed without any possibility of censorship, downtime, fraud or third-party interference.

3 What is the Future of Ethereum?

As Ethereum is on the verge of more updates in their network, we can see in future Ethereum network would be more secure as it is moving from proof of work to proof of stake and it is working on the scalability of network through various updates known as Sharding, Casper, plasma. So after the updates usability of ethereum will get increases.
