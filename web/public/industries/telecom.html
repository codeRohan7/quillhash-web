Key Issues in Telecom

High Cost

Intermediaries like clearing house and roaming agreements increases the roaming cost for the subscriber.

Cost of agreement

Every operator needs to set up roaming agreement which is time consuming and expensive.

Lack of visibility

One provider is not able to connect to all locations.

Settlement costs

In roaming partner settlements, current processes take a month and issue resolution timelines go up to two months.

Core Benefits of Blockchain in Telecom

Smart Contracts

Smart contract to offer services and get paid for service.

Blockchain Client

A blockchain client which will be used by operators to interact directly with subscribers, other operators, and service providers.

Consensus Engine

Based on high transactions we can either choose tendermint or graphene.

Mobile app

A mobile app which will be used by subscribers to view operators and subscribe to the services of operators without changing the sim card.
