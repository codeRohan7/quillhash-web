Stablecoin
Development Company

Stablecoins is a class of cryptocurrencies which offers: instant processing and security payments of cryptocurrencies, and the volatility-free stable valuations of fiat currencies

Start a Project

Features of Stablecoin

  • Scalability and trust
  • Decentralized
  • Easy Exchange
Stablecoins on the other hand, leverage the benefits of cryptocurrencies — such as transparency, security, immutability, digital wallets, fast transactions, low fees, and privacy ,without losing the guarantees of trust and stability that come with using fiat currency.
By enabling a decentralized system that is secure and stable, everything from cross-border lending to financial planning could benefit. With decentralized lending, for example, stablecoins could help ensure a reliable environment for P2P transactions to take place without needing to use a volatile a Cryptocurrency like Bitcoin to transact.
Stablecoin provide a safe haven for the crypto holders in a market in exchanging their Cryptocurrency to a stablecoin without having to change Cryptocurrency to fiat which some crypto platforms don’t allow.

Our Stablecoin based solutions

Commodity backed Stablecoin

They are backed by other kinds of interchangeable assets, such as precious metals. The most common commodity to be collateralized is gold — however, there are also stablecoins backed by oil, real estate, and baskets of various precious metals. Holders of commodity-backed stablecoin holders hold a tangible asset that holds some value.

Fiat backed Stablecoin

The most common type of stablecoins are collateralized, or backed by fiat currency like USD, EUR, or GBP. The value of stablecoins of this type is based on the value of the backing currency, which is held by a third-party regulated financial entity.

Cryptocurrency Backed Stablecoin

They utilize algorithms to control the stablecoin’s money supply, similar to a central bank's approach to printing and destroying currency. Seigniorage-based stablecoins a less popular form of stablecoin.

Day-to-day Payments

Frequently Asked Questions


1 What are stablecoins?

Stablecoins are a new class of Cryptocurrency that was created to provide stability in price and are backed by a reserve asset. Stablecoins are designed to tackle the inherent volatility seen in Cryptocurrency prices.

2 Why do we need Stablecoins?

As the prices of most the cryptocurrencies are highly fluctuating over time, stablecoins provide the stability in prices which directly backed by the reserve of assets. So, Stablecoins give owners a safe place to store their assets whenever there are choppy waters in the crypto world. Consumers can quickly and easily convert from unpegged cryptocurrencies to stablecoins when they are worried about where the markets are heading next, eliminating the need to return to a fiat currency. These conversions can also be less expensive than when switching between crypto and fiat, as it takes the transaction fees of payment processing providers and banks out of the equation.

3 What will be the future of stablecoins?

As the stablecoin is heaven in the crypto world provides quite amazing features. As in 2019 the most popular stablecoin tether achieved an all-time high of daily transactions — and according to CoinMarketCap, the stablecoin is even nipping at the heels of bitcoin, with reported trading volumes of $9.4 billion compared to BTC’s $10.2 billion. And now Facebook have introduced their own stablecoin ‘Libra’ and many other stablecoin projects on the way, so the future looks positive for stable coin.
