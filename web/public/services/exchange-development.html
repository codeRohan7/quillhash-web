Exchange Software Development

A Cryptocurrency Exchange is a platform through which users can buy, sell or exchange digital tokens (cryptocurrencies) against the current market price.

Start a Project

About Exchange Software Development

It also allows the opportunity to individuals who can invest into other coins on the market through the act of trading, much like a stock exchange.

Centralized Cryptocurrency Exchange

A Centralized Cryptocurrency Exchange is similar to a traditional stock exchange or a bank where all the transactions are passed through a central authority which is in control of the funds.

A Centralized Exchange is extremely user friendly because of data being stored in a single central location with features such as password recovery.

  • Higher Trade Volumes
  • High Liquidity
  • Allows fiat-to-cryptocurrency trades
  • Extremely User Friendly with high functionality

Decentralized Cryptocurrency Exchange

A Decentralized Cryptocurrency Exchange does not depend on a central authority for the transactions. Instead the transactions are carried out by automated processes without any central presence in peer-to-peer or customer-to-customer manner.

A Decentralized Exchange offers great security against hacks owing to the data being stored in distributed nodes.

  • No control of a central authority because of distributed nodes
  • Increased User Anonymity and privacy
  • Highest levels of security
  • Globally accessible

White Label Cryptocurrency Exchange

A White Label Cryptocurrency Exchange is a platform with easily customizable frontend with core backend and database functionality already built in. A White Label Exchange can be launched very quickly as the core functionalities are already built.

A White Label Exchange saves a lot of money and time as it can be deployed quickly. The features and functions of our White Label Exchange have been perfected over the time with relevant feedback on performance and usability.
